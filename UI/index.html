How NeuroFace AI Works

Three simple steps to transform your attendance management system.

1

Enroll Students

Capture and encode student faces using our secure enrollment system. Each face is converted into a unique mathematical signature.

2

Real-Time Detection

When class begins, the webcam automatically detects and matches faces against the enrolled database in milliseconds.

3

Mark Attendance

Attendance is automatically recorded in the database with timestamps, ready for instant reports and analytics.
